### Fonts

##### Logo font

FF Enzo Bold

##### Site Font

Roboto

Base scaling 1rem: 10pt

##### Styles & Sizes
- Button
  - 1.6rem
  - Bold
  - Primary orange, dark gray, or white (depending on state)  
- Label
  - 1.2rem
  - Bold
  - Dark gray


##### Icon Fonts

Stars
- 1.8rem
  - icon-star
    - 0xe800
    - Primary orange (highlighted), light gray
  - icon-star-half-fill
    - 0xe801
    - Primary orange
